Best MMA Fighters Of The Year(2022)

Here, We'll Be Showing You The Absolute Best Of The Best That The UFC Has Had To Offer Us This Year(So Far)


In this article, you'll be seeing both fighters that have been on the spotlight for years and still continue to shine as of today, and those that have come to shine as the year rolled in.



1 - Israel Adesanya: "The Last Stylebender"

Israel Adesanya is the middle-weight champion. He is a born-nigerian fighter who moved with his family to New Zealand as a teen. He is considered to be one of the best strikers in the UFC.

2 - Kamaru Usman: "The Nigerian Nightmare"

Kamaru Usman is the welter-weight champion. He is a nigerian-amererican fighter with a background in wrestling.

3 - Charles Oliveira: "Charles Do Bronx"

Charles Oliveira is the former light-weight champion. He is a brazilian fighter, specialized in Jiu-Jitsu. He currently holds the title for the most number of submissions in the history of the UFC. Charles is eagerly awaiting the chance to regain his championship belt, which was stripped off of him after he failed to make weight for a fight.

4 - Francis Ngannou: "The Predator"

Francis Ngannou is the current heavy-weight champion. He is from Cameroon. Francis holds the tittle for the most powerful punch in the UFC today. He is considered to be on of the scariest fighters of all time.

5 - Amanda Nunes: "The Lioness"

Amanda Nunes is both the Feather-weight and Banta-weight champion. She is a brazilian fighter and is considered by many (including the writer of this article) to be the greatest female fighter of all time..
